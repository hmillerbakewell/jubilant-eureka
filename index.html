Click the word at the center of the web! You get one guess. All the words in the grid are related to the target word by a series of at most two links. Valid links are:

  • Synonyms: The two words share a meaning.

    Frank and Blunt both admit a variety of meanings, but are directly linked because "to speak frankly" and "to speak bluntly" are synonymous.

  • Hyper- and Hypo- nyms: One word is an example of another word.

    Sublime (the verb) is an example of Change.

Each word has a fixed meaning, so if the word Frank (meaning to stamp) is used as a link then it will always mean the act of stamping, and never, for example, speaking frankly. Any words used as linking words are also present in the grid.

You get full marks for guessing the center word. You get half marks if your guess was one step from the center. You get no marks if your guess was two steps from the center.

This game uses Wordnet for the chains of meanings (citation below), and these have not been verified by the author.
